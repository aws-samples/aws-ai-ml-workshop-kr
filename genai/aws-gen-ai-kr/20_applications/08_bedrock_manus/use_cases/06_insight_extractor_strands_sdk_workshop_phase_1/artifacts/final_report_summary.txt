
# Moon Market Sales Performance Analysis Report

## Executive Summary

This report presents a comprehensive analysis of Moon Market's sales performance based on data from January to June 2024. The analysis focuses on sales trends, product categories, customer demographics, and promotion effectiveness to provide actionable insights for business growth.

### Key Findings:

1. **Total Revenue**: 8,619,150 KRW from 1,220 orders with an average order value of 7,065 KRW
2. **Seasonal Performance**: May showed exceptional performance with 35% higher sales than average
3. **Product Mix**: Fruit category dominates with 46% of total sales and highest average order value
4. **Customer Demographics**: 30-50 age groups represent 85% of total sales
5. **Promotion Effectiveness**: DAIRYPROMO and FRUITPROMO5 are the most effective promotions (37% of sales)

## Detailed Analysis

### 1. Temporal Sales Analysis

Monthly sales show variability with a significant peak in May (1,834,730 KRW), which is 35% above the average monthly sales of 1,436,525 KRW. The lowest performing month was February with 1,301,850 KRW in sales. The average month-over-month growth rate was 5.16%.

### 2. Category Performance

The fruit category is the clear leader with 3,967,350 KRW in sales (46% of total), followed by vegetables at 2,389,700 KRW (28%) and dairy products at 2,262,100 KRW (26%). Fruits also have the highest average order value at 9,583 KRW, compared to 6,500 KRW for dairy and 5,218 KRW for vegetables.

### 3. Customer Demographics

Sales are relatively balanced between genders, with male customers accounting for 54% (4,655,800 KRW) and female customers for 46% (3,963,350 KRW). The 30-39 age group is the largest segment (35% of sales), followed by the 50-59 age group (29%) and the 40-49 age group (21%). The highest spending demographic segment is males in their 50s.

### 4. Promotion Analysis

Promotions are highly effective, driving 95.5% of total sales. The most successful promotions are:
- DAIRYPROMO: 1,602,000 KRW (18.6% of total sales)
- FRUITPROMO5: 1,593,650 KRW (18.5% of total sales)
- SUMMERFRUIT: 1,455,200 KRW (16.9% of total sales)

Orders with promotions have a 95.8% higher average value (7,369 KRW) compared to orders without promotions (3,764 KRW).

### 5. Price and Volume Analysis

There is a moderate positive correlation (0.38) between quantity and order amount. The average price per item varies significantly by category: fruits (6,157 KRW), dairy (4,003 KRW), and vegetables (3,259 KRW). Average order value increases with quantity across all categories, indicating volume purchasing behavior.

## Recommendations

1. **Seasonal Strategy**: Replicate May's promotional strategy in other months to boost sales during lower-performing periods.

2. **Product Focus**: Expand fruit product offerings and develop targeted marketing campaigns for this high-performing category.

3. **Demographic Targeting**: Develop specialized promotions for the 30-50 age demographic while creating strategies to attract younger customers.

4. **Promotion Optimization**: Focus marketing efforts on high-value promotions like DAIRYPROMO and FRUITPROMO5 while phasing out underperforming ones.

5. **Pricing Strategy**: Implement volume-based discounts to encourage larger purchases across all categories, particularly for fruits which show the strongest volume purchasing behavior.
